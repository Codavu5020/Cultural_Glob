Protect Cultures, Preserve Humanity

🌍 Facts

UNESCO reports that nearly 40% of languages worldwide are endangered, with one disappearing every two weeks.

“Every language is an old-growth forest of the mind.” — Wade Davis, Anthropologist

According to Pew Research (2023), 72% of global internet users consume media from Western sources, often unaware of local alternatives.

✊ Call-to-Action

  • Support local creators by sharing indigenous music, films, and art.
  • Use hashtags like #CulturalVoices and #HeritageMatters to amplify underrepresented cultures.
  • Push for diverse representation in media, education, and policy.
  • Explore endangered languages and traditions, then educate others.
